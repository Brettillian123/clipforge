How it works

Click a clip — it just plays. Add text/shapes/chat from the toolbar and they appear instantly on the video. Drag to move, drag the gold dot to resize, use the floating bar for color/size.

Length / trim: drag the gold handles on the bar under the video to extend or shorten; drag the middle to slide. Use ➕ Insert a part… to splice another moment from your stream into the clip — pick the section, drag where it lands, and choose whether to keep its audio.

Edits autosave. The video you see is a live preview; click Download to render the final file with everything burned in (a badge warns you when the download is behind your edits).
