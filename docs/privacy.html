Privacy Policy — ClipForge

Last updated: June 30, 2026

ClipForge is a personal desktop application that helps its operator create short video clips from their own stream recordings and upload them to their own social media accounts (YouTube and TikTok). It runs entirely on the operator's own computer.

Information we collect

ClipForge does not collect, store, or transmit personal information to us or to any third party. When you connect a YouTube or TikTok account, the resulting access tokens are stored locally on your own device and are used solely to upload videos you choose, to your own account, at your direction.

Use of platform APIs

ClipForge uses the YouTube Data API and the TikTok Content Posting API only to upload videos created by the operator to the operator's own connected account. It does not read, collect, or share data belonging to any other user. ClipForge's use of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

Data sharing

We do not sell, share, or transfer any data to third parties.

Data retention and deletion

All credentials and tokens are stored locally on the operator's device and can be removed at any time by disconnecting the account in the app or deleting the application's local data folder.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Reach out on TikTok, Twitch, or YouTube.

ClipForge is a free, personal, open-source tool. It is not affiliated with YouTube, Google, or TikTok.
